How To Split Test Your Way To a Successful Campaign in Meta Ads

Have you ever had a hard decision in your business or life & wanted a clear answer? You may write up a list of pros & cons. Maybe you decide to get another pair of eyes to give you their opinion. Well, in this blog, we’d like to discuss how to decide between 2 different yet similar variables. It’s called split testing. What is split testing & why do marketers use A/B testing to achieve exceptional results? The idea of split testing is simple. It’s been used in data science ever since we coined the phrase Data science. You decide what variable you want to split test, which will be advertised to a pre-selected audience & given a daily budget. Then after a few days/weeks, you will gather enough data to determine a clear winner.
