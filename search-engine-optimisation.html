There are various important elements of ON-page

1. Content
2. Keywords
3. HTML
4. Architecture

1. Content

Search engine marketing is highly charming upon the creation of useful content regularly. From this point, content may satisfy needs, basically by filling in information or answering a question.


2. Keywords

Keywords direct to the subject or topic of a content piece that is the foundation of a search question, keyword research, and picking are closely tied to content creation and fundamental to SEO marketing efforts. To boost search engine marketing, companies should choose reasonable keywords, both in terms of search commonness and competitiveness.


3. HTML

Optimization of particular HTML elements can help enhance the search ranking of a website or page.


4. Architecture

It is another imperative factor of ON-page SEO. when optimizing websites or pages for SEO benefits, the following architecture aspects are really important.

Site map:
Making a suitable site map of all pages on a specific site can help in crawling efforts and also boost search engine marketing.

Loading speed:
Your pages should load quickly in order to rank your website higher.

Mobile friendly
Your website should be mobile-friendly in order to boost its ranking. Most search engines will punish websites that are not optimized for a streamlined mobile experience.
