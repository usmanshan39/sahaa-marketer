Pinterest Marketing That Makes The Difference

Our professional team works to improve ROI and target new audiences

Promoted Pin Ads combine naturally within the search results, category finder, and home news feed of appropriate users.

Targeting clients on Pinterest can use a mixture of keywords (like Google Ads) as well as interests (like Facebook Ads). We will integrate audience-centric campaigns relying on the particular goal of your campaign. Expert analytics identify the most creative and popular key terms to target but this can still be much more particular than internet-based targeting.

At our site, our Pinterest Ads Managers will also enhance your audience based on the information we collect from understanding and getting to know your product including demographic data. This data-driven approach makes sure that your products are always appearing and appealing to an interested audience.

Promoted Pin Ads statistically perform much better than organic results which can assist in fast-track your product through the sales funnel. They are also a wonderful opportunity to grow product awareness at a low cost and qualify users for retargeting.

Rich Pins are one step further than promoted pins. This Ad format allows products the opportunity to show even more information to a user by incorporating catalogs and metadata on your site.

This gives users an optimized image of information about the pin they are seeing, heightening engagement and improving product awareness. This can influence shoppers, visitors, and pinners to click through your product and convert.

Buyable pins are the Pinterest Ad format that allows users to fast-track through from discovery to purchase and are generally the most focused Ad format within the platform.

Statistically, Pinterest users consume more money per user than other social media platforms. Using comprehensive targeting and refined audience analytics, our experts are able to capture those ready-to-convert clients and maximize your ROAS.

With current performance analytics, we will also examine how users behave and interact with product content. This data can be worked to optimize and entice the client and continuously suit business KPIs.
