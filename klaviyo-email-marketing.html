Klaviyo vs Mailchimp

What is the key difference?

You have heard of both Klaviyo and Mailchimp; undoubtedly the most famous platforms for Email Marketers all around the world. And, whilst they have many resemblances, there are subtle differences between the products that you should believe in your decision.

Both platforms let Email Marketers send Emails automatically. Separate their lists into different segments, run A/B tests, and Create Email Signup forms. However, the pricing framework for the platforms varies particularly.

Mailchimp is a costly option for small businesses that plan to evolve instantly through the use of multiple features. This is because, unlike Klaviyo, not all of Mailchimp’s parts are formed from the jump sneaky!

Both platforms are created to be highly user-friendly. Mailchimp’s interface guides users through the procedure of generating an email which makes it ideal for small business owners who want to set up simple campaigns themselves. The platform itself is perfect for service businesses who learn on their Email Marketing platform for things like inquiry responses and newsletters.

On the other hand, Klaviyo is the home of the more experienced Email Marketer, the people who know what they want to get and the features they will require to get it, with highly customizable templates, costly segmentation, and complete platform integration, Klaviyo is every eCommerce Email Marketer’s dream whether you want to win back to your clients who have not purchased for a long time or upsell complementing brands to recent purchasers, Klaviyo can help you.
